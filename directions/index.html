Getting to Halifax from the Airport

Halifax Stanfield International Airport (YHZ) is located in Enfield, approximately 35 km north of downtown Halifax. Several options are available to get from the airport to Dalhousie University.

  • Halifax Transit 320 Airport Shuttle — A direct shuttle service operates between YHZ and downtown Halifax (Scotia Square - walkable to any peninsula hotels). At the airport, $4.75 tickets can be purchased at the Ground Transportation Booth in Arrivals (or via the HFXGO app or exact change on board). The journey takes approximately 45–60 minutes depending on stops. See schedule for details.
  • Taxi — Taxis are available outside the arrivals terminal. The fare to the Dalhousie campus is typically $70 and the journey takes around 35–45 minutes.
  • Rideshare (Uber) — Uber operates in Halifax. Pick-up is available from the designated rideshare zone at the airport (near the Halifax Transit bus stop). Fares are comparable to taxi rates although vary with surgecharging.

Getting Around Halifax

  • Walking — Downtown Halifax is safe, compact, and walkable area.
  • Buses — Halifax Transit operates bus routes across the city. Several routes serve the Dalhousie campus from the downtown area and beyond. The Halifax Transit website has full schedules and a trip planner.
  • Rideshare (Uber) — As mentioned above Uber operates in Halifax.
  • Parking — For those bringing their own vehicles parking information is available from Dalhousie University and from the Municipality of Halifax.

Places to Stay

The following hotels are within easy reach of the Dalhousie Studley Campus. We recommend booking early as Halifax is a popular summer destination.

  • Dalhousie University Summer Accommodations — Dalhousie provides a number of affordable bookable rooms within Halifax (note: ensure you book a location that is within Halifax and not Truro).
  • The Lord Nelson Hotel & Suites — 1515 South Park Street — The closest full-service hotel to campus, approximately a 5–10 minute walk. Located directly across from the Halifax Public Gardens.
  • Atlantica Hotel Halifax — 1980 Robie St — Next closest standard hotel to campus, approximately 15–20 minute walk. Located by Quinpool Road.
  • Four Points by Sheraton — 1496 Hollis Street — Approximately a 20–25 minute walk but with a small uphill. Located by the Halifax Waterfront next to other Marriott offerings.
  • HI Halifax Hostel — 1253 Barrington Street — A budget-friendly option in downtown Halifax approximately 20–25 minutes walk from campus.

Short-term rental options (e.g., Airbnb) in Halifax’s South End and downtown neighbourhoods are also abundant and often offer convenient proximity to campus.
